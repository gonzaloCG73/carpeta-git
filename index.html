The series called b>Rick and Morty follows the misadventures of cynical mad scientist Rick Sanchez his kind but restless grandson Morty Smith, who divide their time between domestic life and interdimensional adventures that take place in an infinite number of realities, often traveling to other planets and dimensions to through portals and in Rick's flying saucer.
The general concept of Rick and Morty is based on two conflict scenarios: domestic family drama and an alcoholic grandfather dragging his grandson to his children.

Created by Justin Roiland and Dan Harmon
